About TeeRadar

TeeRadar WA makes it easier for golfers to find, compare, and open official booking pages for public courses across Western Australia from a single place.

Our goal

We noticed that booking golf in WA often means jumping between different course websites, different booking systems, and different ways of showing available times. TeeRadar brings those into one clean view so players can see their options faster.

How it works

You choose the date, time window, number of players, and 9 or 18 holes. We look up the course’s public booking page and send you straight there to complete the booking. Pricing and final availability always come from the course site.

What’s next

Right now we’re focused on WA public courses. The structure we’ve built lets us expand to other states and to more booking providers (MiClub, Quick18, Chronogolf, etc.). As more courses give us API access, the experience becomes even more real-time.
