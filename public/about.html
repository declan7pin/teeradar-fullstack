About TeeRadar

Your one-stop platform for finding live tee times and discovering WA’s best public golf courses.

Our Mission

At TeeRadar WA, our goal is simple — make golf booking effortless, transparent, and connected. Instead of searching through multiple club websites, we bring WA’s public golf courses together in one intuitive platform, helping players find live tee times, filter by 9 or 18 holes, and book directly through the official course pages.

Our Vision

We believe golf should be accessible to everyone. By simplifying the booking process and supporting local clubs, we aim to help more people play the game they love — from weekend beginners to seasoned golfers.

Our Future

TeeRadar is expanding beyond Western Australia. We’re building towards a national platform where every golfer in Australia can find, compare, and book rounds in just a few clicks. Expect more features soon — including analytics, smart filters, and exclusive local course insights.
