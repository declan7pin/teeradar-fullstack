Refunds Policy

Last updated: 24 November 2025

1. Overview

TeeRadar WA is a tee-time search tool that shows public golf courses in Western Australia and then sends you directly to the course’s official booking website (MiClub, Quick18, or the club’s own site).

We do not take bookings, process payments, or charge green fees. All payments, cancellations and refunds are handled by the course or platform you book with.

2. TeeRadar does not process payments

Because all bookings happen on each course’s official system:

  • TeeRadar WA does not collect your payment information
  • TeeRadar WA cannot charge your card
  • TeeRadar WA cannot issue refunds
  • TeeRadar WA cannot modify or cancel your tee time

3. How to request a refund

If you need to request a refund or change a booking, you must contact the course or booking platform you paid through.

MiClub bookings: Refunds, cancellations and changes follow the individual club’s policy. Visit the club’s MiClub page for instructions.

Quick18 bookings: Refunds are handled by the club operating the Quick18 page. Look for their cancellation/refund rules during checkout.

Direct course websites: If the course uses its own website or phone bookings, contact the club directly to request a refund.

4. Course refund policies may vary

Each golf course sets its own rules around:

  • Cancellation deadlines
  • No-show fees
  • Wet-weather refunds
  • Rescheduling
  • Credit or voucher policies

TeeRadar WA has no control over these rules.

5. Incorrect availability or price

If a course price or slot is incorrect on the course’s official system, the club may adjust or deny the booking according to their own terms. TeeRadar WA cannot override course decisions.

6. When to contact us

You should contact TeeRadar WA only if:

  • A course link is broken
  • A course is listed incorrectly
  • You experienced a technical problem with the TeeRadar website

Email: teeradar.help@gmail.com

We will answer quickly, but we cannot modify or refund external bookings.

7. Changes to this page

We may update this Refunds Policy if new features are added or if legal requirements change. The “Last updated” date will be revised when updates occur.
