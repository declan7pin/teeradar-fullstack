Frequently Asked Questions

What does TeeRadar do right now?
We show WA golf courses on a map, try to read their public booking pages, and send you straight to the course’s own booking page to finish the booking.
Why are most courses in Western Australia?
We started with WA because many public/resort courses use similar booking systems (MiClub, Quick18, etc.). Once we have traffic and course-level approval, we can add other states.
Why do I need to subscribe?
Guests can browse the map, but subscribers get direct booking links and future features (saved home course, faster lookups). Intro price is $5/month for 3 months, then $10/month.
Can I get a discount?
Yes — the owner can add certain emails to a discount list. If your email is on the list, the login page will show your discounted rate.
Some courses say “phone bookings only”. Why?
Those courses don’t expose an online timesheet. We still show them on the map so golfers know they exist, and we give the phone number so you can call.
Why does the availability check take a few seconds?
When you click Search, we visit each course URL in your WA list and try to read the times for the date you chose. That’s 15–20 websites sometimes, so we show a loading bar while that runs.
Does TeeRadar actually book for me?
No. We send you to the course’s own booking page with the right date and course. This keeps the course in control and makes API approval easier later.
Can I set my home course?
Yes — that feature is being wired into the booking page so the map can centre on your home course automatically.
What happens if a course URL changes?
Then it may show as unavailable. You can update the course JSON in the backend to fix it.
How do I contact you?
You can add a “Contact us” page later — for now, update the footer or FAQ with your preferred email.
