Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about searching, subscriptions, and bookings.

What is TeeRadar?

TeeRadar helps you find and compare tee times across Australia, then sends you to each course’s official booking page to complete your reservation.

How does booking work?

Use filters and the map to find a time. Click Proceed to booking to open the official course booking page for your date (and time when supported).

Do I need a membership?

Guests can browse; members (A$10/mo or A$99/yr) unlock direct links and faster updates.

Is pricing final?

We surface prices from provider pages where possible; final pricing is confirmed on the course’s checkout page.

Can I cancel my membership?

Yes — cancel anytime from the Dashboard. Access continues until the end of the current period.

Will you add one‑click bookings?

We plan to integrate partner APIs where approved. Until then, proceed links take you to the official course page.

How can courses get listed?

We’re rolling out state by state. Course operators can contact us to connect their booking system.
