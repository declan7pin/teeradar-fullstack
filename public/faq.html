Frequently Asked Questions

Do I book on TeeRadar?

No — TeeRadar shows you courses that match your filters and then sends you straight to the course’s official booking page. That way pricing, payment, carts and conditions are always up to date.

Why do I have to log in?

We block guests from going past the home page so we can keep scraping/collecting course availability without abuse. Logging in also lets us remember your preferences and later show ad-free or discounted access.

Why does it sometimes say available when the course page is different?

We read the course’s public timesheet formats (MiClub, Quick18, etc.). If the course changes their page layout or adds reCAPTCHA, we may only be able to show the course but not confirm the exact slot. In that case, click through to the course page.

Which courses are included?

We started with public courses in WA — including Araluen, Collier Park, Whaleback, The Cut, Secret Harbour, Sun City and others — and we’re expanding. The map will keep growing as we add more booking links.

Will this support all of Australia?

Yes — the structure already supports expanding to other states. Once we have enough usage to request official API access from the course platforms, we can make it more real-time and national.

Why do some courses show a phone number instead?

A few WA courses only take bookings over the phone. We still show them on the map so golfers know they exist, and we display the phone number directly.

Does TeeRadar charge me?

Right now we’re testing access free. Later we may add optional subscriptions for ad-free access and extra features.
